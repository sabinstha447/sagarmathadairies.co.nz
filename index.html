About Our Farm

Established in 1950, Green Pastures Dairy has been a cornerstone of our community, committed to traditional farming methods combined with modern, ethical practices. Our family-run farm prides itself on the health and well-being of our cows, ensuring they graze on lush, nutrient-rich pastures year-round.

We believe that happy cows produce the best milk. That's why we prioritize their comfort, providing ample space, fresh water, and a stress-free environment. This dedication translates directly into the superior taste and quality of our dairy products.

From our farm to your table, we ensure every step of the process adheres to the highest standards of quality, sustainability, and animal welfare. Join us in celebrating wholesome dairy, made with care and passion.

Meet Our Herd

Daisy

Daisy is one of our oldest and wisest cows, known for her gentle demeanor and consistently rich milk. She loves a good scratch behind the ears.

Bessie

Bessie is a playful and energetic young cow, always curious and eager to explore new patches of grass. Her milk is incredibly sweet.

Molly

Molly is our resident matriarch, calm and majestic. She's a fantastic mother and her lineage consistently produces our highest quality dairy.

Our Delicious Products

Farm Fresh Milk

Our flagship product: pure, pasteurized, non-homogenized milk, straight from our happy cows. Available in whole, semi-skimmed, and skimmed.

  • Rich in calcium
  • No artificial hormones
  • Naturally delicious

Artisan Greek Yogurt

Thick, creamy, and packed with live cultures. Perfect for breakfast or as a healthy snack. Available in plain, honey, and berry flavors.

  • High protein
  • Probiotic-rich
  • Creamy texture

Golden Farm Butter

Churned from the freshest cream, our butter boasts a rich, golden color and an unparalleled taste. Ideal for baking or spreading.

  • Grass-fed cream
  • Rich, natural flavor
  • Perfect for cooking
